About Uplift

What is Uplift?

Uplift helps organizations identify growth opportunities to eliminate waste, set clearly defined targets, and measure progress while working toward the ultimate goal of continuous delivery.

The model charts a clear path that can be completed in stages and allows flexibility for progressing through five key dimensions of software development: Code, Build & Test, Release, Operate, and Optimize.

How It Works

The model serves as a "cloud readiness" gauge that quantifies how close a product is to being ready for migration. This is achieved by establishing targets for a subset of capabilities that define the minimum requirements. Teams who own legacy products get a clear goal to work toward that they can easily track, allowing organizations to operate in a decentralized, self-service way.

FAQ

What are the dimensions?

Products are evaluated across five categories: Code (development practices), Build & Test (quality assurance), Release (deployment capabilities), Operate (production management), and Optimize (continuous improvement).

What is the scoring scale?

Each capability is scored from 1 (initial/ad-hoc) to 4 (optimized/measured). Higher scores indicate more mature practices.

What is the Cloud Readiness Score?

The overall percentage reflects how many capabilities with defined minimum thresholds are meeting those minimums. A 100% score means all cloud-readiness requirements are met.

Can I customize the model?

Yes! The model is defined in YAML configuration files that can be easily modified to meet your specific organizational needs. You can change capability descriptions, add new capabilities, or adjust minimum thresholds.
